Privacy Policy

Effective 17 June 2026

Who we are

MaxAds ("we", "us") is an advertising-optimisation tool operated by devupnow. Contact: privacy@maxads.ai.

What data we access

When you sign in, we access your basic Google account profile (email). With your explicit authorisation (OAuth), and only via the Google Ads API under the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/adwords scope, we access your Google Ads account data — campaigns, ad groups, budgets, performance metrics, search terms, and (if you connect it) Merchant Center data — to provide the service.

How we use it

We use this data solely to (a) analyse your account and generate optimisation recommendations you review, and (b) apply the specific changes you explicitly approve. We do not use it for advertising, we do not sell it, and we do not use it to train generalised AI models.

Limited Use

MaxAds' use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only access the scopes necessary to provide and improve MaxAds' features to you, and we do not transfer or use this data except as needed to provide the service, to comply with applicable law, or as part of a merger or acquisition with your consent.

How we store and protect it

OAuth refresh tokens are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM using a per-account, cloud-KMS-wrapped data-encryption key; they are never logged or shared. Your account data is tenant-isolated (row-level security) and accessible only to you and the service operating on your behalf. We operate on least privilege and request only the scopes we need.

Sharing

We do not share your Google data with third parties, except infrastructure subprocessors strictly necessary to run the service (e.g. cloud hosting and database providers), bound by confidentiality, and never for their own purposes.

Retention and deletion

You can disconnect your Google account at any time, which revokes and deletes your stored tokens. You may request full deletion or an export of your data at any time (privacy@maxads.ai); we honour deletion promptly.

Your rights

Consistent with the GDPR and the Swiss FADP, you may request access to, correction of, export of, or deletion of your personal data.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy on this page with an updated effective date.
