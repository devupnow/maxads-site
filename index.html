MaxAds

Stop wasting Google Ads spend — with changes you approve.

MaxAds is an AI co-pilot that reads your Google Ads account, finds high-confidence optimisations, and applies them only after you approve. Nothing changes behind your back.

What it does

Connects read-only first

Link your Google Ads account; MaxAds analyses performance — campaigns, budgets, search terms, conversions — without touching anything.

Finds what matters

A statistical engine surfaces only opportunities it can back with evidence: wasted spend, budget-constrained campaigns, and negative-keyword candidates.

You approve every change

Each recommendation is a specific, reversible action shown with the numbers behind it. You approve it; MaxAds applies it — and you can undo it in one click.

How it works

  1. Connect your Google Ads account (read-only).
  2. Review the AI's recommendations, each with the evidence behind it.
  3. Approve the ones you want — applied to your account, reversible.

Why teams trust it

  • Nothing is applied without your explicit approval.
  • Read-only "shadow mode" lets you preview before enabling changes.
  • Bank-grade security: your Google credentials are encrypted with per-account keys and never shared.
