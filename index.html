MaxAds turns your structured product data into high-performing Google Ads campaigns, then optimises them against the KPIs that actually matter to your business. Every change is yours to approve — and fully reversible.

What it does

Builds campaigns from your structured data

Connect your Merchant Center and Google Ads account. MaxAds uses your product feed and account structure to build and shape campaigns — starting with Performance Max.

Optimises on the KPIs that matter to you

Set your real goals — ROAS, CPA, profit, blended MER — and MaxAds optimises toward them, surfacing only changes it can back with evidence.

Full Google Ads management

One place to build, optimise, and manage your account. Performance Max today; Search, Shopping, and Display next — the same approve-and-apply workflow across every campaign type.

You approve every change — reversibly

Each recommendation is a specific action shown with the numbers behind it. You approve what you want; MaxAds applies it to your account, and you can undo it in one click.

  1. Connect your Google Ads and Merchant Center accounts (read-only to start).
